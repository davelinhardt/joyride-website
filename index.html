How it works

Two sides. One marketplace. Joy on both.

01

Drivers subscribe.

One flat monthly fee on the Pro plan gives drivers access to the marketplace, dispatch, payments, and support. No commission on Pro. No surprises.

02

Drivers set the price.

Drivers publish their rates. Riders see real, transparent pricing — no algorithmic surge math.

03

Riders book the ride.

Riders pick a driver they trust, save favorites, and pay through the app. The fare goes straight to the driver.

04

Both sides win.

Drivers earn more. Riders get better service. Repeat trips build relationships, not metrics.
