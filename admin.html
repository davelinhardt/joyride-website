Settings

App-wide knobs. Changes take effect on the next time the rider/driver clients open the active-ride screen.

Wait-time grace period

How long the driver waits at pickup before the rider starts being charged a wait fee. During this window the rider's Wait Time stat counts down from this value and Wait Fees stays at $0.00. After it expires, Wait Time counts up and the fee accrues.

Subscription Plans

Monthly subscription fee and transaction fee % per plan. Transaction fee % is the cut Joyride takes from each ride's Total Fare (e.g. 7 means 7% per ryde). Drivers see these values on Profile → Subscription Plans & Billing; the transaction fee % also drives the per-ride Transaction Fee on every Ride Detail breakdown.
