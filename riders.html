Frequently asked

Rider questions, answered.

How is Joyride different from Uber or Lyft?

Drivers run their own businesses on Joyride. They pay a flat monthly subscription instead of giving up 25–40% of every fare. You see real driver profiles, real prices, and you choose who you ride with.

Can I still book a ride right now?

Yes — you'll see drivers available near you and can book in seconds, just like you'd expect. You can also schedule rides ahead.

Are prices higher than Uber?

Often lower or about the same — and there's no surge multiplier. Drivers set transparent rates so you can compare and pick what works for you.

How do I save a driver as a favorite?

Tap ♥ on their profile after a trip. They'll show up at the top next time, and you can book them directly.

What about safety?

All drivers are background-checked, vehicles are inspected, and every ride is GPS-tracked with in-app emergency support. Driver profiles show real verified reviews from past riders.
