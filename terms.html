05Charges, payment, and refunds

How rates are set. Joyride is a marketplace. Drivers set their own rates. The rate you see when you request a ride reflects the Driver's published per-minute rate, applicable multipliers (for example, scheduled rides, long trips, or early-morning pickups), and any applicable airport surcharges or taxes. Joyride does not impose surge pricing.

Fare calculation. Fares are calculated based on the transport portion of the trip — the estimated minutes from pickup to drop-off as returned by our routing provider — multiplied by the Driver's per-minute rate, with any applicable multipliers applied, and subject to a minimum fare. Pickup time is not billed to the Rider. The fare quoted to you at booking is the fare you will pay, adjusted only for changes you request during the ride (for example, an added stop or a destination change) or for applicable taxes, tolls, and surcharges.

Payment. Joyride uses third-party payment processors (currently Stripe) to charge your stored payment method for fares, tips, cancellation fees, no-show fees, tolls, airport surcharges, taxes, and other amounts you authorize. By providing a payment method, you authorize us and our payment processor to charge that method for amounts you incur through the Joyride Platform. If a charge fails, you remain responsible for the amount owed and we may retry the charge or use another payment method on file.

Tips. You may, but are not required to, add a tip after a ride is complete. One hundred percent (100%) of tips go to the Driver. Tips are not subject to Joyride's transaction fee.

Refunds and adjustments. All charges are final unless otherwise required by law. If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact us within thirty (30) days at riders@joyride.cool. We may, in our discretion, issue a partial or full refund or credit toward a future ride. Adjustments granted in one case are not a commitment to issue adjustments in other cases.
